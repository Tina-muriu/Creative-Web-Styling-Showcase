Market liquidity is an overlooked topic when it comes to trading. The definition of liquidity is; “the availability of liquid assets to a market or company”. In the simplest way possible, Liquidity is a measure of the ease of ability to enter and exit a market at the desired price based on the number of buyers (bids) and sellers (asks/offers) in that market.
